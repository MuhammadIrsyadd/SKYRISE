01 — Game Concept
Core premise, player fantasy, and unique selling points

The Fantasy

You're a legendary urban developer racing to build the world's tallest skyscraper. Each precisely placed floor earns prestige. One miscalculated drop and your tower narrows — then crumbles. The city is watching.

Core Loop

Watch the block swing → feel the timing → tap to drop → react to feedback. Perfect alignment triggers euphoric fireworks. A miss punishes tower width. Endlessly replayable in 30 seconds or 30 minutes.

Unique Edge

Hyper-realistic architectural façades drawn per pixel on Canvas. The city skyline shifts from golden dawn to neon midnight as you rise. 5 building types with real architectural detail. Combo multipliers that shake the screen.

Placement Accuracy System

If the remaining block width drops below 15% of the original foundation width → Game Over. Excess trimmed pieces fall with simulated physics and shatter on landing.

07 — Visual Effects & Game "Juice"
Feedback animations, particles, audio, and screen events

PERFECT! Burst

Gold + white particle burst from impact point. "PERFECT!" text bounces in, scales up, fades. Screen flashes briefly warm. Crowd cheer SFX. Duration: 0.6s.

Combo Escalation

At 3× combo: multiplier badge pulses. At 5×: score counter shakes. At "Architect Mode": full confetti storm, slow-motion next block, triumphant sting.

Block Trim Physics

Cut-off excess piece rotates and falls with simulated gravity. Dust/debris particles on cut edge. Satisfying "slice" sound effect. Piece shatters when hitting distant ground.

Camera Shake

On MISS: light screen shake (4px, 200ms). On large miss (>60% overhang): strong shake + red screen tint flash. Game Over: extended shake + dramatic SFX.

Environment Transitions

Sky color smoothly interpolates between time-of-day palette stops. Background city layers shift speed. Window lights in background buildings gradually appear as night falls.

Audio Design

Web Audio API. Ambient city hum layers with height. Each block type has unique placement "thud." Perfect = chime. Great = click. Miss = creak. Crowd noise builds with combo.

Screen Transitions

Game Over: tower sways, slow-motion block falls, cinematic zoom out revealing full tower height. Score summary slides in. High score celebrated with ticker tape + trumpet fanfare.

Accessibility

prefers-reduced-motion respects OS setting (animations disabled). High-contrast mode toggle. Touch + click + keyboard (Space) all trigger block drop.
